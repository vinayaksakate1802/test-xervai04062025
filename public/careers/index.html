Careers at XervAi

At XervAi, we believe that every great idea starts with a passionate mind. We’re not just looking for someone to fill a role—we’re inviting you to join a team that values what you bring to the table and helps you grow every day.

Imagine working somewhere where your expertise in Azure, AWS, PowerShell, Python, CyberSecurity, or Terraform isn’t just required—it’s celebrated. At XervAi, you’ll have the opportunity to transform complex infrastructures into clean, scalable, and secure environments. We take pride in solving challenging puzzles like tidying up AD forests, making MFA work reliably, and migrating legacy systems without a hitch.

We offer more than a job. Our team thrives on collaboration, genuine respect, and the freedom to share new ideas. We believe that when you’re empowered and your voice is truly heard, innovative solutions happen—and that means you get to see your influence in every project we deliver. Flexibility, balance, and trust are the cornerstones of our culture; we understand that great work comes when your schedule respects your life.

If you’re looking to be part of a company that puts people and progress first, where every contribution can shape the future, then XervAi is the place for you. Join us, and be part of a team that values you.
